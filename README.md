  ## About BackTime

  ## FAQ                                                                                                                                                                                  
                                                                                                                                                                                          
  **How do I start a timer?**                                                                                                                                                             
  Tap the play button to start your segment timer.                                                                                                                                        
                                                                                                                                                                                          
  **How do I add a break?**                                                                                                                                                               
  Tap the + button next to the break time display.                                                                                                                                        
                                                                                                                                                                                          
  **Does it work with Apple Watch?**                                                                                                                                                      
  Yes! The companion Watch app syncs automatically with your iPhone.
